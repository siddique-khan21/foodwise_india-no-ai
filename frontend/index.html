Platform Rules & Guidelines

Ensuring safety, quality, and trust for all users

Donor Rules

  • Only donate safe, edible food
  • Provide accurate food details and timing
  • Maintain proper storage temperatures
  • Coordinate promptly with receivers
  • Ensure proper packaging for transport

NGO/Receiver Rules

  • Only accept assigned donations
  • Follow proper pickup protocols
  • Provide timely feedback to donors
  • Maintain hygiene standards
  • Distribute food promptly after receipt

Volunteer Rules

  • Only assist approved pickup requests
  • Maintain professional conduct
  • Respect donor and receiver privacy
  • Report issues immediately
  • Follow safety guidelines during transport

Compost/Animal Feed Farm Rules

  • Only collect non-edible donations
  • Maintain hygiene and safety standards
  • Follow environmental regulations
  • Provide quality compost/feed
  • Coordinate pickup schedules efficiently
